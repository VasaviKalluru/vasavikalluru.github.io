Hello and welcome to my page!

I am Vasu (Vasavi Kalluru) and I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Social Work with a specialization in mental health from Tata Institute of Social Sciences, Mumbai. I did my undergraduate degree in psychology with an honors from the University of Delhi and I am deeply passionate about the mental health of marginalised communities. I am trained in and deliver workshops on the issues of caste, gender, sexuality, mental health and disability. As a Dalit trans person in higher academia, my anti-caste feminist values heavily guide my work to understand human behavior, and the complexities of mental health issues.
